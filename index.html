League Bylaws

League Structure

The league consists of two tiers: the Champions League and the Development League, each with 10 teams.

Promotion & Relegation

The bottom two teams in the Champions League are relegated to the Development League each season. The top two teams in the Development League are promoted to the Champions League.

Midseason Tournament

A 20‑team single elimination tournament begins after Week 4 and continues through Week 10. Seeding is based on league standings at the time the bracket is created.

Playoffs

Each league has an 8‑team playoff beginning in Week 15. Final standings determine champions and promotion or relegation outcomes.

Trades, Waivers & Rosters

Teams follow Sleeper roster settings and FAAB waiver rules. Trades and transactions are processed through the Sleeper platform.

For rule questions, contact the commissioner through league chat.
