League Bylaws

Public Safe

Article I — Contacting League Officers

Use league chat for commissioner questions. Personal contact details are omitted from this public page.

Article II — Fees

  • Champions League: $100 + $25 tournament fee
  • Development League: $75 + $25 tournament fee

Article III — Payouts

Champions League pays top three plus weekly regular-season prizes. Development League pays top four.

Article IV — Fee Deadline

All fees are due before the draft.

Article V — Midseason Tournament

20-team single-elimination event seeded after Week 4 and managed manually outside Sleeper.

Article VI — Playoffs

Each league uses an 8-team playoff bracket beginning in Week 15.

Article VII — Draft

Draft order is determined through a weighted lottery and slot-selection process.

Article VIII — Trades

Trades must be accepted through the hosting service and may only be vetoed for cheating or collusion.

Article IX — Promotion / Relegation

Bottom two Champions League teams go down; top two Development League teams go up.

Article X — Rule Creation

League votes are used when a dispute is not covered by existing rules.

Article XI — Roster

1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 Flex, 1 K, 1 DEF, 6 Bench, 1 IR.

Article XII — Oversights

Invalid lineups can lead to penalties.

Article XIII — Scoring

Half-PPR scoring with standard passing, rushing, receiving, kicking, and defense values.

Article XIV — Waivers

FAAB system with a $250 starting budget and playoff FAAB add-on.
