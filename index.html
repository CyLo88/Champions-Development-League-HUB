League Bylaws

Article I — Contacting League Officers

League officers are available if you have questions or need assistance. For league-specific matters, contact the commissioner through league chat.

Personal commissioner names, phone numbers, and email addresses have been removed from this public page.

Article II — League Entry & Other Fees

  • Champions League: $100 league fee + $25 Midseason Madness fee
  • Development League: $75 league fee + $25 Midseason Madness fee

Article III — Prize Payout Structure

  • Champions League: 1st $400, 2nd $200, 3rd $120, weekly prizes $20
  • Development League: 1st $400, 2nd $200, 3rd $100, 4th $50

Article IV — League Fee Due Date

All fees are due before the draft. Any unpaid team before the draft is removed from the league.

Article V — Midseason Tournament

The league hosts a 20-team single-elimination tournament involving all teams from both leagues. Teams are seeded after Week 4, with points scored as the tiebreaker.

Article VI — Playoff Configuration

Each league uses an 8-team playoff bracket beginning in Week 15.

Article VII — Draft and Draft Order

The draft occurs between preseason Week 3 and the start of the regular season. Draft slot selection is determined through a weighted lottery process.

Article VIII — Trades

Trades must be accepted through the hosting service to count and may only be vetoed for cheating or collusion.

Article IX — Relegation and Promotion

The bottom two Champions League teams are relegated and the top two Development League teams are promoted.

Article X — New Rule Creation

When a dispute is not covered, the league discusses options and then votes. Approved changes apply to both leagues.

Article XI — Roster

  • 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 Flex, 1 K, 1 DEF, 6 Bench, 1 IR

Article XII — Roster Oversights

Managers must start a full valid lineup each week. Oversights can result in a 10-point deduction.

Article XIII — Scoring System

The league uses half-point PPR scoring with standard passing, rushing, receiving, kicking, and defense values.

Article XIV — Waivers

The league uses a FAAB waiver system with a $250 starting budget. Playoff teams receive an extra $50 FAAB for the playoffs.
