League Overview

The Champions & Development League Hub tracks a 20-team fantasy football ecosystem split into two 10-team divisions. At the end of each season, the bottom two Champions League teams are relegated and the top two Development League teams are promoted. The league also features a separate midseason tournament managed outside Sleeper.

How This Site Works

Standings and matchup data pull from Sleeper. The midseason tournament bracket is controlled manually through a file in GitHub, which means only the site owner can edit the matchups while everyone else can view the published bracket.

League Bylaws

Article 1 — League Structure

The league is made up of two tiers: the Champions League and the Development League. Each league has 10 teams, creating a 20-team system built around annual movement.

Article 2 — Promotion & Relegation

At the end of each season, the bottom two teams in the Champions League are relegated to the Development League. The top two teams in the Development League are promoted to the Champions League for the following season.

Article 3 — Midseason Tournament

The league hosts a 20-team single-elimination tournament outside of Sleeper. The bracket is created manually and seeded from league results. Tournament matchups can still be displayed on this site using manually entered pairings and published results.

Article 4 — Playoffs

Each league uses an 8-team playoff format beginning late in the season. Playoff results determine league champions and help shape promotion and relegation outcomes.

Article 5 — Standings & Tiebreakers

Teams are primarily ordered by record. Points scored can be used as a major tiebreaker and are displayed on this site for added clarity.

Article 6 — Rosters

League roster settings, lineup requirements, bench rules, and IR slots are managed through Sleeper. This website reflects public roster and standings information only.

Article 7 — Trades

Trades are processed through Sleeper and can be summarized on the transaction tabs for each league.

Article 8 — Waivers & Free Agency

Waivers and free agency follow your Sleeper league setup. The site surfaces transactions for visibility but does not control them.

Article 9 — Scoring

Scoring is determined by the active Sleeper settings for each league season. Matchup scores displayed on the site reflect Sleeper’s reported totals.

Article 10 — Commissioner Communication

For any rule interpretation, disputes, or changes, contact the commissioner through league chat. Personal contact information has been intentionally omitted from this public page.
